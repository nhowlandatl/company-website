Our History

"Die Könige der Brezeln"


In 1913 the German newspaper Brezelzeit called us "Die Könige der Brezeln" or in English, The Kings of Pretzels.

For our founders, Helga Brezelesser and Gottlieb Brezelesser, this award brought with it the pride of knowing they served the best pretzels in all of Stuttgart, Germany.

From that small store on the side of the road, the Brezelessers decided to open a new shop that would catapult them into the big-time world of pretzel-making.









From then to Now

Now our pretzels are sold wherever shoppers shop around the world. If you listen closely, every few seconds you will hear the crisp snap of someone biting into one of our delectably tasty pretzels!

We are a company that cares about our customers. Every product we package is meant to fill our customers and those around them with joy!
